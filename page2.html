Hepatobiliary and gastroenterology

 

Progression over time, gastrointestinal and hepatobiliary diseases and dysfunction have an increasing trend, may be due to life stress, dietary changes and infections (such as Helicobacter pylori), plus various carcinogenic factors, such as gastric cancer, colorectal cancer, etc. It has listed the top ten cancer death causes in China. Moreover, Taiwan is a high-risk area for viral hepatitis. Hepatitis, cirrhosis and liver cancer are also common diseases, and the disease needs to be followed up.

 

In order to develop into a sub-specialty of service and academic considerations, the undergraduate course gradually develops and introduces various new types of diagnostic and therapeutic technologies, and is committed to delving into the clinical and drug use of liver diseases. In addition, it has deepened the community to conduct health education and screening, and strengthen the implementation of services. The concept of the community.
